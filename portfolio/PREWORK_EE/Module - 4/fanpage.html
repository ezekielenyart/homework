DCapella Fanpage


DCappella began their journey on Amerian Idol in 2018. To date, they have completed a 40-city tour of North America as well as selling out an 18-city tour in Japan.

Members of DCappella

DCapella consists of 7 members in seven vocal parts

  • Antonio Fernandez - Vocal Percussion
  • Joe Santoni - Bass
  • Orlando Dixon - Baritone
  • RJ Woessner - Tenor
  • Kelly Denice Taylor - Alto
  • Kalen Kellly - Mezzo
  • Morgan Keene - Soprano
